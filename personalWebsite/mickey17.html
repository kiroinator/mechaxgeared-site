MICKEY 17 / MICKEY BARNES

Mickey Barnes is "Expendable", he is used in an illegal process where he takes on lethal tasks and a new clone of him with restored memories is created after each death. After so many deaths, he becomes Mickey 17.Do not ask him how it's like to die. Nasha's boyfriend.

THOUGHTS

I love sci-fi so much and honestly this movie was a great addition to that! It's a very interesting concept and it made me want to read the book it's based off of, maybe one day when I have time. The messege in this movie is very clear/in your face about it, which I don't mind. Robert Pattingson has crazy vocal range and dare I say a good actor. Everyone did so well in this movie tbh, Mark Ruffalo was a pleasant surprise despite the character he played. The Niflheim scenery was sooo pretty and I loved the space and spaceship shots. Creepers were so cute...overall great movie. Freaky, but still a great movie.
