SUBNAUTICA

Subnautica was released in early access for Windows in December 2014, macOS in June 2015, and Xbox One in May 2016. The game was released out of early access in January 2018 for macOS and Windows, with versions for PlayStation 4 and Xbox One in December 2018. The physical console versions were published by Gearbox Publishing. The Nintendo Switch, PlayStation 5, and Xbox Series X/S versions were released in May 2021. It received generally positive reviews from critics and sold over 5 million units by January 2020. A spin-off, Subnautica: Below Zero, which was originally meant to be downloadable content for the base game, was released in May 2021. A direct sequel, Subnautica 2, is planned to release in early access sometime in 2025. - wikipedia

I only just started playing Subnautica a couple weeks ago and I LOVE IT! I was watching a youtuber play this game, which I'll mention down in media, and I thought huh, i forgot how much i wanted to play this game in 2015. It looked like a silly survival fish game that seemed fun. Anyway I downloaded it and its superrr fun!! I love the sea and fishies and aliens and this game is a mix of all that. I'll admit I'm not great at combat or finding the other escape pods in game, I'm more of a scavenging person. I've also been focussing on building habitats and upgrading my stuff rather than exploring too deep...its not like i can manage anyway. I already died trying to explore a deep cave, lost some materials. I'm moving rather slow at the game but its nice to just look around sometimes. Beautiful game, really.
